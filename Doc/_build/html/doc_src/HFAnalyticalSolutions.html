Hydraulic fracture propagation Analytical solutions notably for

  • planar radial fracture with constant injection rate
  • height contained fracture
HFAnalyticalSolutions.HF_analytical_sol(regime, mesh, Eprime, Q0, muPrime=None, Kprime=None, Cprime=None, length=None, t=None, Kc_1=None, h=None, density=None, Cij=None, gamma=None, required='111111')[source]¶

This function provides the analytical solution for the given parameters according to the given propagation regime

Parameters:
  • regime (string) –
    • K (toughness dominated regime, without leak off)
    • M (viscosity dominated regime, without leak off)
    • Kt (viscosity dominated regime , with leak off)
    • Mt (viscosity dominated regime , with leak off)
    • PKN (height contained hydraulic fracture with PKN geometry)
    • E (elliptical fracture propagating in toughness dominated regime).
  • mesh (CartesianMesh) –
  • Eprime (float) –
  • Q0 (float) –
  • muPrime (float) –
  • Kprime (float) -- the fracture toughness (K') –
  • Cprime (float) –
  • length (float) – axis in the case of elliptical fracture and the fracture radius in all of the rest).
  • t (float) –
  • Kp_perp (float) –
  • h (float) –
Returns:

t (float) – time at which the fracture reaches the given length. r (float) – length of the fracture at the given time (fracture length in the case of PKN, length

of the minor axis in the case of elliptical fracture and the fracture radius in all of the rest).

p (ndarray-float) – pressure at each cell at the given time. w (ndarray-float) – width at each cell at the given time. v (float) – propagation velocity. actvElts (ndarray) – list of cells inside the fracture at the given time.
